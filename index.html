About WeeDoo?

WeeDoo is our short term for “WE DO IT FOR YOU” it was a web platform that aims to give convenience to businesses and its customers using a QR code in their mobile devices. It was a solution for businesses like, any restaurants, hotels, resorts and to any related establishment for social distancing.

It is an online application that enables diners to scan a QR code and access establishment menu using a mobile device. It simply allows customers/guest to easily make an order instantly at the store. Once the order is confirmed, you just need to wait for your food to be served instantly. It is equipped with all the functions you need to have a very convenient unique dining experience.
